MantleScope is ready to use the moment you open it in a browser. There is nothing to install beyond the MetaMask extension. Once your wallet is connected, MantleScope fetches your Mantle balances, pulls live prices from CoinGecko, and computes an AI risk score — all within a few seconds.

<Note>
  Your private keys and seed phrase never leave your browser. MantleScope reads your balances using the MetaMask injected provider; no data is sent to any MantleScope server.
</Note>

## Connect and get your first risk score

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the MantleScope app">
    Navigate to the MantleScope app in your browser. You will land on the connect screen, which shows the MetaMask option and a **Continue with demo data** link.

    <Tip>
      No wallet? Click **Continue with demo data** to explore all features — dashboard, positions, simulator, and Vault Guard — using a preloaded portfolio. Demo mode simulates live block updates and price movements so you can evaluate the tool before connecting.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your MetaMask wallet">
    Click the **MetaMask** button. MetaMask will ask you to approve the connection request — click **Connect** in the MetaMask popup.

    If your wallet is currently on a different network, MantleScope automatically calls `wallet_switchEthereumChain` to move you to Mantle Mainnet (Chain ID 5000). If Mantle is not yet in your wallet, it calls `wallet_addEthereumChain` to add it first. See [Connect MetaMask to MantleScope](/connecting-wallet) for the full network configuration details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your dashboard">
    After connecting, MantleScope immediately:

    - Fetches your MNT and ETH balances from Mantle Mainnet
    - Pulls live MNT and ETH prices from CoinGecko
    - Resolves your ENS name (via Ethereum mainnet) and displays it in the top bar
    - Computes your AI risk score (0 – 100) and renders the risk ring
    - Generates AI insights based on your portfolio concentration and current prices
    - Populates the Alerts feed with any active risk events
    - Starts auto-refresh: prices every 30 seconds, block number every 4 seconds, balances every 60 seconds

    The Dashboard shows your total portfolio value, MNT and ETH balances, liquidation risk, concentration percentage, and a live AI insight. Use the sidebar to navigate to **Positions** for per-asset health factors or **Alerts** for the full alert feed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up a Vault Guard">
    Navigate to **Vault Guard** in the sidebar. Vault Guards are autonomous onchain contracts that act when a condition is met — for example, pausing a position if MNT drops below a price threshold.

    Click **+ New guard** to open the guard modal. Select:

    - **Asset to protect** — MNT, mETH, USDC, or MNT/USDC LP
    - **Trigger action** — Pause position, Alert only, or Withdraw to wallet
    - **Trigger condition** — Price drops below, health factor below, concentration above, or risk score above
    - **Threshold value** — The numeric trigger value
    - **Protocol** — Agni Finance, Lendle, Mantle LST, or Init Capital

    Click **Deploy vault guard on Mantle** to create the guard. Every guard action is recorded in the **Proof Log** as an immutable onchain transaction.

    See [Vault Guard](/features/vault-guard) for a full explanation of guard contracts and supported trigger types.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the risk score means

MantleScope calculates a score from 0 to 100 based on your current MNT price and portfolio concentration:

| Score range | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 70 – 100 | Low risk | Healthy concentration and price levels |
| 40 – 69 | Medium risk | Elevated concentration or price pressure |
| 0 – 39 | High risk | Significant exposure; vault guard recommended |

The AI insight panel below the risk ring explains the key driver of your current score and suggests a vault guard trigger price when risk is elevated.
