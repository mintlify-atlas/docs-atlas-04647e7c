## What MantleScope does

MantleScope brings together real-time blockchain data, live price feeds, and an AI risk engine into a single dashboard. When you connect your wallet, MantleScope immediately scans your Mantle positions, computes a risk score from 0 to 100, and surfaces actionable insights — all without your private keys ever leaving your browser.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open MantleScope">
    Navigate to the MantleScope app. If you don't have MetaMask installed, you can explore all features using **Demo mode** — no wallet required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Click **Connect** and approve the MetaMask prompt. MantleScope will automatically switch your wallet to Mantle Mainnet if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your risk score">
    Your portfolio is scanned instantly. Check your risk score on the Dashboard, review individual positions, and set up Vault Guard rules to protect your assets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  MantleScope operates entirely in your browser. Your private keys and seed phrase are never transmitted to any server.
</Note>
